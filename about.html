About Us

Camiluz Enterprises, Inc. is a top dried mango manufacturer in the Philippines since 1973. Our premium products use only the finest Philippine mangoes and are made using a traditional, decades-old process developed over the years by our founders. Our GUADALUPE brand is crafted from carefully-selected ripe mangoes, with no artificial coloring or flavoring. Our commitment to quality has made us one of the most trusted brands of dried mango products in the Philippines.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve the legacy of Philippine mangoes by producing exceptional dried mangoes in Cebu. We seek to share joy across generations through products rooted in culture, craftsmanship, and dedication to excellence.
